The closed cloud · the open way

The closed cloud

  • Closed-source, paid, and cloud-only
  • Locked to a single model vendor
  • No agent choice, no self-hosting, no BYOK
  • Around $500 a month for the privilege

The open way

  • Apache-2.0 — fork, self-host, go commercial
  • Bring your own keys, any compatible endpoint
  • 21 coding agents, auto-detected, zero config
  • Around $73 a month — over 80% less
