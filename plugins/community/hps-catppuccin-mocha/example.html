The problem

Frontier design,
locked in the cloud.

In April 2026, Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — produce real design artifacts, not write about design. Yet it shipped closed-source, paid, and cloud-only. Open Design sets that capability free.

  • Locked to one model vendor — no agent choice
  • No self-hosting, no local files, no repo context
  • No BYOK — your keys and endpoints don't apply
